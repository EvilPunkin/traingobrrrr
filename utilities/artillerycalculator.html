Usage

  1. Make sure the tank is stationary and on level ground.
  2. Enter the initial cannon direction, in degrees. This can be gotten by looking straight ahead along your tank and then using f3 to get the bearing.
  3. Enter the amount of blocks the cannon is offset from the seat. Press f3 to get your coords while sitting in a seat, and then enter those in the initial coordinates.
  4. Enter the coordinates of the target.
  5. Enter the amount of powder charges you want to use.
  6. Press Calculate.
  7. After getting the degrees, input them into your cannon. If you're just eyeballing the rotation of the cannon, use the global space rotation. If you're putting them in through computercraft or whatever, use the local space rotation.
