Very Specific Probability Calculator

This calculator is used to solve a probability question that goes something like this:

You pick N balls out of an infinite urn filled with K different colors of balls. What are the chances of getting one of each of the different colors?

Why did I make this? Because it took a while to find the right equation to solve this and I decided to immortalize it in code. Hopefully this actually is the right equation, or I'll be looking like an even larger idiot than normal.
